Support the Karmapa Foundation Europe

Generosity is the key

Each of us learnt about the Karmapa and his incommensurable activity through the generosity of others. His Holiness plays a key role to keep alive the transmission of Buddhist teachings preserved for centuries in Tibet in a pure form. Without the dedicated effort and financial support of those who felt inspired to share these teachings, the previous Karmapa would never have been in Europe and the outreach of Buddhism would not be of the current scale, making much more difficult meeting authentic spiritual masters. Moreover, the 17th Karmapa is very much involved in projects (ranging from the preservation of the teachings and the Tibetan culture to the preservation of the natural resources) that need funds to run.

This is where you come in

We would like you to join us in sharing these precious teachings with the world. The most important thing you can do is to bring these teachings into your life, but it is also very important to contribute financially. The amount you donate is up to you. Even €10 a month will make a big difference, and you will have added your contribution to the stream of support we receive from around the world.
