Teachings

Teachings of His Holiness the 17th Karmapa have not yet taken place in Europe; however, one of the objectives of the KFE is to facilitate the first visit of HH to Europe which will result in the first teachings in Europe. Meanwhile there is already a rich material for those who would be interested in teachings of HH which were implemented outside Europe.

THE HEART IS NOBLE: CHANGING THE WORLD FROM INSIDE OUT His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa
The book is available around the globe in English as a hard cover or e-book. It can be ordered directly from its publisher Shambhala, Amazon or other bookstores. The Spanish translation is already available. Coming later in 2013 Russian and French.
TRAVELING THE PATH OF COMPASSION: A COMMENTARY ON THE THIRTY- SEVEN PRACTICES OF A BODHISATTVA His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa
KTD Publications, Woodstock, NY. 2009. Series of teachings given by His Holiness in Tilokpur Nunnery, India. Offers deeply inspiring yet eminently practical guidance in walking the bodhisattva path in the 21st century.
THE FUTURE IS NOW: TIMELY ADVICE FOR CREATING A BETTER WORLD
Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa Hay House, Carlsbad, Calif. 2009. Pairing contemporary photography with 108 sayings on how to live more consciously, offers advice on themes as the environment, freedom, responsibility, loneliness and contentment.
HEART ADVICE OF THE KARMAPA
Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa, Altruism Press, Sidhpur India. 2008 Collection of talks given by the Seventeenth Karmapa on an array of topics, including appreciating the environment, applying loving-kindness and compassion, understanding karma, and enjoying harmony with other religious traditions.
NGONDRO FOR OUR CURRENT DAY
Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa KTD Publications, Woodstock, NY. 2010. Guides students through the four traditional preliminary practices, based on a shortened practice text especially composed by the Karmapa for students seeking to engage in such spiritual practice in the context of busy work lives.
KAGYU MONLAM BOOK: A COMPILATION FOR RECITATION
Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa Altruism Publications, Sidhbari, India. 2009. Collection of prayers and practices for use at the annual Kagyu Monlam Chenno. In the non-sectarian spirit of the Seventeenth Karmapa, this draws together texts from a wide variety of Buddhist traditions.
CEREMONY OF OFFERING TO THE GURUS
Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa Author: Seventeenth Karmapa KTD Publications. Woodstock, NY. 2007. Offering to the gurus (gurupuja) composed by the Seventeenth Karmapa in response to the needs of people today, with the aim of making the practice accessible to all.
DEER PARK CALLIGRAPHIES
Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa KTD. Woodstock, NY. 2004. The book comprises beautiful reproductions of 19 original calligraphies drawn by the Seventeenth Karmapa during a stay in Sarnath, India.
ENVIRONMENTAL GUIDELINES FOR KARMA KAGYU BUDDHIST
Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa MONASTERIES, CENTERS AND COMMUNITY Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa Downloadable at www.khoryug.com Guidance in implementing His Holiness’ vision of caring for the environment as a way of caring for beings.
MUSIC IN THE SKY; THE LIFE, ART AND TEACHINGS OF THE 17TH KARMAPA OGYEN TRINLEY DORJE
Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa Author: Michele Martin Snow Lion. Ithaca, NY. 2003. Offers detailed account of His Holiness’ childhood and escape from Tibet, as well as selections of his early poetry and teachings.
DANCE OF 17 LIVES: THE INCREDIBLE TRUE STORY OF TIBET’S 17TH KARMAPA
Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa Author: Mick Brown Bloomsbury USA. 2005. Details the extraordinary story of the exiled Tibetan teenager, who has been hailed as one of the greatest spiritual leaders of the coming age.
KARMAPA: THE POLITICS OF REINCARNATION
Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa Author: Lea Terhune Wisdom Publications. 2004. Biography of the Seventeenth Karmapa recounts his escape from Tibet, as well as the political aftermath. Explores the process whereby the Karmapa was recognized, as well as the splitting off of a faction backing another candidate.
KARMAPA: URGYEN TRINLEY DORJE
Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa Author: Ken Holmes Altea. 1995. Presents information on such topics as the Karmapa’s lineage, reincarnation, the land of Tibet and Tibetan Buddhism itself.
HIS HOLINESS THE XVIITH GYALWANG KARMAPA
Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa Karma Lekshey Ling Publications, Nepal Photo essay on the early years of the Seventeenth Karmapa’s life, from the details of his search and discovery through 1992. Photos supplemented with a concise biography.
CELEBRATING KARMAPA
Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa KTD Publications, Woodstock, NY. 2010 Documents the Karmapa’s 2008 return to his North American seat in New York.
THE LIFE OF MILAREPA
Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa DVD, 255 minutes. Universal Music Taiwan, 2010. Exquisitely produced DVD records the live dramatic performance of a play written and produced by the Seventeenth Karmapa. Drama combines elements of traditional Tibetan opera and modern theatre, for a world-class theatrical event staged in Bodhgaya.
KARMAPA IN AMERICA 2008: WISDOM OF ENLIGHTENED MIND
Author: His Holiness the Seventeenth Karmapa 3 DVDs | Karmapa Foundation USA. 2009. Fresh and personal first teachings by His Holiness on Western soil. Speaking directly to modern hearts and minds, the Karmapa shares his insights on developing compassion, wisdom and working with hardships.
