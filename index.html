Letter from His Holiness Gyalwang Karmapa Ogyen Trinley Dorje (16 September 2013)

I wish to offer my sincere appreciation on the successfull formation of the Karmapa Foundation in Europe. I look upon the Foundation as the organisation devoted to facilitating my religious, charitable, educational and allied activities within Europe. As such, it supports me in fullfilling my responsabilities as a spiritual leader to care for both the European dharma centres and the disciples, as well as in other activities and projects across Europe.

I hope that all our dharma centres in Europe can work together with the Karmapa Foundation to help it accomplish its dharma mission, and, thereby, successively promote not only the teachings of Lord Bouddha but also peace and harmony in Europe and the world, through our actions and intiatives. It is essential that we work collectively to remove any obstacles that arise which might impede our religious and charitable aims and objectives.

I am grateful for all of the founding members and staff of the Karmapa Foundation for the work and thought they have put into the creation of this organisation, and I pray that all its efforts for the welfare and good of sentient beings may bear fruit.

  • Passing Away of Dr. Choje Akong Tulku Rinpoche

    9 October 2013 Brussels.

    It is with deep sadness that the Karmapa Foundation Europe (KFE) announces the death of Dr.Choje Akong Tulku Rinpoche. Rinpoche was assassinated in Chengdu (China) yesterday morning, together with his attendant Chime and one of his nephews.

    The reasons behind this mad gesture are still unknown. The KFE expresses its heartfelt condolences to the members of Rinpoche's family and to all his students.
